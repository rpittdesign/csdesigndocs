DESIGN

Design Elements

Colors

Primary Colors

  • Primary colors are used on calls to action, links and buttons
  • They should be used only as a final draw of attention

Secondary Colors

  • Secondary colors are here in the off chance that a call to action needs less refence
  • Can also be used for header elements to be identified separately

Greys

  • Used primarily in text and to emphasize or de-emphasize content

Typography

1.0 HEADER - H1

Interaction

  • Only to be used for primary page headlines
  • Level of importance is equal to logo
  • Limit to 1 per page for screen readers & SEO

Styles

  • max-width: 600px - font-family: Lato-Bold; - font-size: 36px; - color: #333333; - letter-spacing: 0.4px; - line-height: 50px
  • sentence case
  • text-align: center;

2.0 HEADER - H2

Interaction

  • Typically only used on headers for major sections in widgets
  • Try to limit to no more than two per page

Styles

  • font-family: Lato-Bold; - font-size: 27px;
  • color: #333333;
  • letter-spacing: 0.51px; - line-height: 35px;
  • text-align: center;
  • sentence case

3.0 HEADER - H3

Interaction

  • Used within widgets, emails etc.
  • Most commonly used header
